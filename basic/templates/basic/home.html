About Us

Mind Hunt is social web application which helps the recruiters to decide the right candidate for their company!

Tons of Options

With the help of modern technologies we are building certain techniques which tests the IQ level of the candidate. One of our best technique is by taking psychometric tests which is totally unbiased.

Reliable

This applicationis totally reliable as it works on AI. This has proven to be a boon in the placement season where due to a lack of time and management recruiters find it hard to take certain important tests.

How do we Help?

We have used ML algorithm which helps to group the candidates on the basis of their responses in the tests. This is totally unbiased and reduces the time of evaluation and gives accurate results!
